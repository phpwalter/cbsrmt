CBS Radio Mystery Theater

Broadcast Log for 1982

This log contains the correct numbering for the 1982 season.

"SHOW #" is the number of that unique broadcast not accountijng for reruns. 
The numbering starts at "1" and each unique episode gets its own number.

"OTRW #" is the numbering system that OTRWalter uses. His numbering system 
accounts for the original broadcasts and the reruns. Each broadcast, 
original and rerun, gets its own unique number. This log currently only 
lists the original broadcasts but will eventually have the reruns listed 
as well.

Ken Long - kenglong@thelongtrek.com
May 27, 2007
